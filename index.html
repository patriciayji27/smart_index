Reading the Vol Surface
Beyond a Single Number

Equity index vol surfaces encode rich market expectations — about tail risk, implied correlation, dealer positioning, and sector dynamics — that go far beyond VIX. This project builds a toolkit to systematically extract and track those signals.

The Dispersion Signal
Implied correlation from COR3M isolates whether vol is driven by broad co-movement or idiosyncratic stock moves. The correlation smile (COR10D − COR90D) leads VIX by 1–2 weeks at regime transitions.
✓ Researched & Published
○
Dealer Gamma Feedback Loop
Dealer gamma positioning creates a feedback loop that mechanically compresses SPX realized vol below implied — endogenously generating a large portion of the observed vol risk premium. The gamma flip point is estimable.
✓ Researched & Published
◇
The Tech Premium Signal
The software/SPX spread encodes real-rate sensitivity, risk appetite, and positioning crowding simultaneously. Tracking its widening and tightening against implied vol ratios and the correlation signal may reveal regime transitions before they register in VIX.
